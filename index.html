Real Estate Logistics

What we do — clean sourcing, clear confirmation, fast coordination.

Larmah Enterprise helps people and businesses move smarter across Nigeria by combining two essential pillars: Verified Real Estate and Trusted Logistics. We reduce stress and fraud by using a trust-first workflow — clear references, simple communication, and quick confirmations on WhatsApp.

Verified Real Estate (Shortlets • Rentals • Sales) We source and coordinate verified options based on your dates, budget, and preferred area. You get clear pricing, availability confirmation, and next steps without confusion.
Logistics (Dispatch • Pickup • Haulage • Freight) From same-day dispatch to heavier freight support, we coordinate movement with pickup/drop-off clarity, ETA updates, and handling notes — all in one thread.
Scam-resistant process We prioritize clean confirmations, traceable refs, and structured communication to reduce mistakes and fraud. You always know what you’re paying for and what happens next.
WhatsApp-first support Bookings and movement updates stay in one place — faster decisions, fewer mix-ups, and smoother execution.
