The problem

Premium clients need more than a public contact form.

Access control

Premium users should enter through a secured dashboard, not an open public page.

Organised requests

Requests need to be grouped, tracked and reviewed according to the business pillar.

Admin verification

New users need administrator approval before sensitive client-level actions are trusted.
